How to approach a hungry population or find your next meal
Consider the below
For soon-to-be eaters:
Remember that there might not be people that will get your goods immediately, but don't worry, we've got people on the case.

There are deliverers constantly searching for new jobs to complete, so you won't be alone for long.
No one is going to rip you off. We have both a secure PIN system and the ability to track the deliverer's info so they can't cheat you out of a deal. Your safety is our top priority.
For the prospective deliverer:
I'm going to say this bluntly at first, cars make everything easier. We know you might want to demonstrate your marathoning skills in front of the buyer, but save them the pain of waiting a long time after accepting. Although props to the running athleticism.
Be sure to have a phone that can access the internet. Also, don't harass or intimidate the people who want a late-night snack. Really. We'll ban you and possibly get the authorities involved if you are being that irrational.
